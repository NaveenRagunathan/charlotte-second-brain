# How to Sell your High Ticket Service in an Unpredictable Market

A 45-Minute Workshop for Founders, Consultants & Coaches Earning $3K-$20K/month - $100

## The Problem
LinkedIn is tougher, finding buyers is twice as hard. But that is a good thing for you to stand out.

You built a great service. So why does growth still feel like guesswork?
- Referrals (when they feel like it)
- Content (you hope someone reads)
- Networking (but your calendar's already full)
- Outreach (you tell yourself you don't need it)

You're relying on Hope Marketing. And hope is not a business strategy.

## What You'll Walk Away With
- A sales process that fits your personality - not the shouty, pushy kind
- How to lead value-rich sales conversations that close without chasing
- The elegant way to bring in buyers - without needing 10X the effort

## For You If...
- You're making $5K-$20K/month but growth is inconsistent
- You secretly avoid outreach because it feels gross
- You're tired of hoping that content or referrals will do the work
- You want to feel confident leading high-value sales conversations
- You're ready to finally take control of your lead flow and revenue
