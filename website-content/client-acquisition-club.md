# Client Acquisition Club

Master LinkedIn, Content, and Sales Strategies to Attract and Close High-Value Clients.

## Core Message
Your reputation should be filling your diary. If it is not, the problem is not your expertise.

The Client Acquisition Club is for consultants, coaches, and fractional executives who are established, bring years of expertise, and are ready to stop depending on referrals by creating a simple system to attract more clients.

## The Problem
- You left corporate with a reputation, track record, and network - so why is finding clients still this hard?
- You spent years/decades where your credibility was never in question
- Then you left to build your own practice and realized the system that opened doors didn't come with you
- Your LinkedIn connections fail to spark meaningful conversations
- Your messaging feels unclear
- You post on LinkedIn, have good conversations that never convert

## The Solution: Client Acquisition Club
