# The Client Acquisition System Scorecard

5 pillars - 15 questions - 5 minutes

## The 5 Pillars

### 1. Positioning & Messaging
Clarity that attracts the right people and repels the wrong ones
- Can you describe exactly who you help and the specific outcome you deliver?
- Would a stranger landing on your website/profile immediately understand what you do?
- Does your messaging speak to a painful, specific problem?

### 2. Lead Generation Engine
Consistent systems that bring qualified leads to you without manual hustle
- Do you have at least one lead source that works whether you post on social media or not?
- Do you know roughly how many leads you need per month to hit your revenue target?
- Do new leads enter through a valuable resource or experience?

### 3. Nurture & Trust Building
Warming leads before asking for the sale so they come to you pre-sold
- After someone enters your world, do they receive value automatically?
- Does your nurture content build trust by teaching and sharing real results?
- Do you have proof points (case studies, testimonials, data) woven into your content?

### 4. Sales Conversion Process
A repeatable system that turns interested leads into paying clients
- Do you have a clear, defined path from interested lead to paying client?
- Do you know exactly what happens when someone says "I'm interested"?
- Do you follow up with leads who did not buy?

### 5. Retention & Referral Loop
Maximising lifetime value so every client becomes a multiplier
- After a client finishes, is there a natural next offer or way to continue?
- Do you actively ask for testimonials, referrals, or introductions?
- Do you stay in touch with past clients through a system?

## Scoring

### Foundation Stage (0-3/15) - Critical gaps
Relying on hustle, word of mouth, or sporadic efforts. Survival mode.

### Building Stage (4-7/15) - Pieces but not a system
Leads come in but do not convert. Clients finish but do not come back.

### Momentum Stage (8-11/15) - Working, now optimise
Leads flowing, clients converting. Gaps are leverage points.

### System Stage (12-15/15) - Running a machine
Attract, nurture, convert, retain without manually driving every step.
