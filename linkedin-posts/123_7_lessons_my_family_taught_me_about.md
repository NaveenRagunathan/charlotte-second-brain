7 lessons my family taught me about: 

Being a better person and running a business:

I’ve been married for 12 years and have 8-year-old twins.

They’re not just my family.

They’re my biggest motivation for everything I do
Including building my business.

1. Patience builds trust.
 My kids have taught me that results take time.
 Whether learning to ride a bike or building a business. 
 Trust the process.
 
2. Be present, not perfect.
 My husband reminds me: Not about doing everything.
 Clients feel this too. Presence creates connection.
 It’s about showing up fully. 
 
3. Curiosity brings opportunities.
 Every “why” from my kids makes me think differently. 
 In business, asking better questions means you offer:
 Better solutions.
 
4. Kindness is strength.
 My husband handles challenges with grace.
 This shows me being kind doesn’t mean being weak
 It’s what sets great leaders apart.
 
5. Celebrate small wins.
 From watching my kids master a new skill 
 To sharing quiet wins with my husband.
 Progress wins over perfection.
 
6. Resilience starts at home.
 My family shows me that even if things get tough.
 We adapt, pivot, and move forward
 Just like in business.
 
7. Love fuels everything.
 My husband and kids remind me why I work so hard: 
 It's for them: love gives it meaning. Same in business.
 A business without purpose is just work.

Which one gets you the most?
