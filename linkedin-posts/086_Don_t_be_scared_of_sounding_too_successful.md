Don't be scared of sounding too successful.

I used to hide my success.

I thought it would make me sound… boring. 

Too corporate. Too “serious.” Too much.

24 years in sales doesn’t make me an influencer. 
(Phew! Cos that was never a goal)

$20m in sales closed is no small feat.

📌 It means I’ve led real conversations that moved $ 
📌 It means I’ve sold across industries and buyer types. 
📌 It means I don’t need trends to stay relevant.

I downplayed ALL of it.

Because on LinkedIn, it started to feel like experience made you less relatable.

I see this every day with the business owners I coach:

→ You’ve worked at Pfizer, McKinsey, LVMH. 
→ You’ve trained at Harvard, INSEAD, Oxford. 
→ You’ve turned around teams and built global brands.

But your content?  Sounds like everyone else’s.
Watered down. Safe. 

Stripped of the very thing that sets you apart.

Your authority isn’t too much.
It’s the conversion power you’re not using. So:

Mention your experience in sales calls. 
Own your history in the DMs.
