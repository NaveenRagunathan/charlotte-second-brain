Hitting $100K this early in the year didn’t happen by chance.

It happened because of intentional shifts — in content, offers, sales strategy AND mindset.

If you’re serious about scaling, here are 5 things you need to focus on:

1. Focus on HUMAN conversations, not just content
Content alone won’t bring clients — conversations will.

Every post should have a purpose:
→ Build curiosity.
→ Start more DMs.
→ Move leads closer to a buying decision.

If your content isn’t sparking conversations, time to switch it up.

2. Raise your prices (even when it feels uncomfortable)
Most coaches undercharge because they price based on what they think people can afford.

Instead, charge based on the value and outcomes you deliver.

Raising your prices attracts clients who are serious about results — not just browsing for information.

Price follows positioning. The sooner you own your value, the faster your business grows.

3. Create offers that scale
1:1 coaching has limits — if you want to scale, you need offers that allow you to:

→ Serve more clients without working longer hours.
→ Build predictable monthly revenue.
→ Focus on growth, not just delivery.

Scalable offers like group programs or masterminds are the key to consistent growth.

4. Sell in your content but not too often.
Vague calls to action like “Message me to learn more” won’t cut it.

Instead, be clear and direct about the next step:
→ “DM me ‘100K’ if you’re ready to scale.”

Sales becomes simple when your audience knows exactly what to do next.

5. Go deep, not wide
You don’t need a massive audience to make massive sales.

Instead, focus on:
-Building trust with your current network.
-Nurturing relationships in the DMs.
-Creating content that is buyer centric.

The key to hitting $100K?
Don't chase attention — turn the attention you already have into clients.
