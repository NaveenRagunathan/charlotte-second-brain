I built my business on LinkedIn at 45,  then exited corporate for good.

Relief.
Excitement.
A quiet kind of conviction.

That’s what I felt when I made the leap.

After 23 years in corporate sales, working with global giants like Danone, PepsiCo, and P&G…

After flying to over 50 countries, advising presidencies from Kazakhstan to Kenya…

I knew how to sell.

But I wanted more than boardrooms and bottom lines.

I wanted to help people who're brilliant at what they do.
But had never been taught how to sell it.

So I started building my business as a side hustle.

Content.
Conversations.
Clients - all from LinkedIn.

15 months later, I exited corporate.
Fully.

And never looked back.

What I’ve learned:

Established coaches and consultants don’t lack skill.
They’ve mastered their craft.
They get results.

But they’re often still relying on:
→ Referrals that dry up
→ Leads that don’t convert
→ Content that builds visibility, but not revenue

What they need now?

→ A refined, repeatable sales process
→ Messaging that moves buyers
→ Confidence to lead every conversation, zero pressure

That’s why I started this business.
That’s why I launched the Client Acquisition Club.
That’s why I’ve made it my mission:

To help coaches and consultants sell with 
clarity, confidence, and calm.

To show them that sales isn’t about pushing.
It’s about guiding.

To teach the strategies and mindset that 
I honed over two decades.

Every time I watch a client land their first $5K, $10K or $100K deal…

It reminds me why I’m here.

Sales changed my life.
Now I help others use it to change theirs.
