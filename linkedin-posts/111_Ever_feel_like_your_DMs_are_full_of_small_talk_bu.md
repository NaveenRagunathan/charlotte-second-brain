Ever feel like your DMs are full of small talk… but no sales?

You’re not alone. 

Most biz owners struggle with one big mistake.
Not knowing how to pivot from casual chat to business.

They either:
❌ Stay stuck in small talk and never shift to business.
❌ Go straight to pitching and get ignored.

The fix? A smooth transition. I call it the SEGWAY.

✔ Acknowledge the convo:
“Excellent you're.... [personal news/commonality].”

✔ Shift naturally:
“How are you finding X? [their business/problem area]?”

✔ Keep it light, not salesy:
“I hear this a lot. Mind if I share how others handle it?”

This makes the shift feel natural.
So they want to keep talking.
