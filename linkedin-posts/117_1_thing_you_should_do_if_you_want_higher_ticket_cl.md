1 thing you should do if you want higher-ticket clients:

(This might sting. Stay with me.)

Stop hiding behind perfect offers, endless content, or “just one more tweak.”

Here’s what you actually need to do:
1. Share your offer, ask for feedback.
2. Ask them about their challenges and listen.
3. Message 10 potential clients every single day.
4. Get on calls, help them for free to prove your value.
5. Repeat this process until you feel strong in your offer.
6. 1 hour crafting an offer that solves a specific problem.
7. Refine your offer based on what they need.
8. Deliver value they can’t help but trust you.

Here’s what most people don’t get:
You don’t need a perfect offer.
You don’t need a big audience.

You need:
→ Confidence built by doing, not planning.
→ Conversations with the right people.
→ Proof your solution works.

Everything else is procrastination disguised as progress.
→ Spending weeks perfecting your website.
→ Tweaking your profile endlessly.
→ Hiding behind content instead of connecting.

→ Building before selling is a trap.
→ Conversations feel uncomfortable
→ But they’re where the magic happens.

When you choose discomfort
It gets you closer to high-ticket clients 
Faster than any “perfect plan” ever will.

Sell first. Build after.

Higher-ticket clients aren’t waiting for your perfect offer.

They’re waiting for YOU to:

-Show up
-Prove your value
-Lead them to the solution they need.
