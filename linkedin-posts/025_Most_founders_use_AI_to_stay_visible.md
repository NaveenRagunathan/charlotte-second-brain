• Your deal coach 
• Your outreach strategist 
• Your discovery call analyst 
• Your qualification filter

The difference isn’t the model.
It’s the questions.

Here are 8 Claude sales prompts I use 
(+ teach founders to use) to build predictable revenue:

1️⃣ Pressure-test your sales pitch 
Find the objections before the buyer does.

2️⃣ Build a 7-touch outbound sequence 
Buyer-centric. Not product-centric.

3️⃣ Stress-test your discovery call 
Expose where you’re leaking authority.

4️⃣ Prepare for a high-stakes sales call 
Walk in with a call plan, not hope.

5️⃣ Qualify whether to pursue a deal 
Stop wasting time on low-probability pipeline.

6️⃣ Write cold outreach that gets replies 
No generic fluff. No “hope this finds you well.”

7️⃣ Map your real ICP 
Not demographics. Buying triggers.

8️⃣ Build a 90-day pipeline strategy 
Activity targets backed by logic.

Claude won’t magically fix your sales.
But it will force you to think like a sales director instead of a hopeful founder.

And that shift alone changes revenue.
