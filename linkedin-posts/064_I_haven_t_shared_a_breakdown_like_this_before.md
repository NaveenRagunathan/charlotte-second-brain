I haven't shared a breakdown like this before 
I figured it was time. 
Since a lot of clients ask me how to:

Scale or diversify their business.

This is how I'm growing my coaching business.
I decided to show up daily on LinkedIn 4 years ago 
I prioritised one other thing - sales.

I’ve spent 20+ years selling in corporate. 

My simple breakdown of how mine works today:

1️⃣ Private membership club - group coaching  (~60%) 
The foundation of my business. 
High-leverage membership with peer to peer learning on sales and LinkedIn for coaches and consultants.
Actionable insights and hands on coaching.

2️⃣ 1:1 Mentorship (~11%) 
For a handful of serious consultants and coaches. 
Premium, focused, and designed for acceleration.
This creates recurring revenue and I cap it to 5 a month.

3️⃣ B2B Consulting (~19%) 
Bespoke projects and training for SMBs. 
High-value, often multi-month contracts.

4️⃣ Brand Partnerships + Keynotes (~10%) 
Speaking, events, and collaborations. 
Credibility + reach + additional income streams.

The 5th? Digital products like workshops and more which I haven't fully dived into yet.

Your mix might look different. 
But the principle stays the same: 
Diversify, simplify, and structure for scale.

And new changes are coming with mine...

Which mix would make the most sense for your business?
