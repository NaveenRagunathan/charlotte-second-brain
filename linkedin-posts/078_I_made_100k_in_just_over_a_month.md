1. I stopped pitching. Started mirroring.
When they say: "I'm overwhelmed with tech" 

Don't say: 
"My program simplifies everything" 
Say: 
"Tell me more about the tech that's overwhelming you"

They feel heard and start to lean in.

2. I never mention its a "12-week program"
No one wakes up wanting to buy a program. 
They wake up wanting $10k+ months. 
They wake up wanting freedom.

Sell the destination, not the vehicle.

3. I killed weak CTAs
OUT: "Let me know if you're interested" 
IN: "Are you ready to fix this now and make it simple?"

Wishy-washy language = wishy-washy results.

4. My high ticket price IS the selling point
Client: "That's a lot of money" 
Me: "That's exactly why it works.” 
Me: “You'd trust a $500 course to get $30K months?"

Premium prices = premium results.

5. Objections are goldmines
"I need to think" tells me I’ve missed something.
SAY: 
"What specifically needs more thought?" 
Call out how they feel in that moment.

Their answer tells me exactly what to address.
