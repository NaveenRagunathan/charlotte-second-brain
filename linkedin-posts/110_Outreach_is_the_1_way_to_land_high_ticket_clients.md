Outreach is the #1 way to land high-ticket clients.

3 things that keep you from getting clients.

Most coaches and consultants overlook this.

And send random DMs, get ignored, and assume “outreach doesn’t work.”

90% my clients come when I initiate a conversation.

What to avoid:

1. Boring, lazy or too direct messages
↳ “Hey [name], I help coaches scale to 7 figures.”
✅ Fix it: Personalize your outreach.

2. Targeting the wrong fits
↳ If your DMs aren’t converting, then wrong person:
✅ Fix it: Be clear on who you serve.

3. Selling too soon
↳ Nobody wants to feel sold to in the first message.
✅ Fix it: Start a real conversation.

And the last? 
Asking a business related question when connecting.

"How is your health routine at the moment?" 
(If you are a health or fitness coach).
