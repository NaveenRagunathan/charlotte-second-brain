You love your kids. 
So why does school drop-off feel like relief?
 
You wave goodbye. 
And then you breathe out.
 
For a moment, it feels good. 
And then the guilt shows up.

Relief doesn’t mean you love them less. 
It means you’ve been carrying a lot.
 
You’re not relieved because they’re gone. 
You’re relieved because you’re not on call for everyone.

That’s the part that matters for business.
 
Loving your kids 
and missing yourself 
can exist at the same time.
 
You’re not a bad parent for needing space.
That tension isn’t failure. It’s care.

The real failure isn’t feeling relief. 
It’s building a business that never gives it back.

I felt that relief this week when mine went back to school.
