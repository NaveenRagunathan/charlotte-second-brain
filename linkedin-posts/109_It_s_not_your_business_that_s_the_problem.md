It's not your business that's the problem, 

It's the story you tell yourself about it.

Most coaches and consultants hate with the sales part.

Not because their offer is bad.
Not because the market is saturated.
Not because “no one is buying.”

Because of the story they tell themselves about selling.

It's easy to say things like:
❌ “I’m just not a natural salesperson.”
❌ “People won’t pay high-ticket for this.”
❌ “I don’t want to sound pushy.”

They hold back.
They avoid selling.
They reinforce the very outcome they fear.

But what if you rewrote the story?

Instead of “I’m not a natural salesperson,” 
→ “Sales is a skill I can master.”

Instead of “People won’t pay high-ticket,” 
→ “I attract clients who see the value in my work.”

Instead of “I don’t want to sound pushy,” 
→ “I guide people to the right decision with confidence.”

Mindset on sales is more of a roadblock than sales itself.

The best business owners aren’t held back by obstacles.
They decide what those obstacles mean.
They reframe them into opportunities.

Sales isn’t about convincing people. 
It’s about leading them.

Change your story, and you’ll change your results.
