Some people inspire you by what they build. 
Others inspire you by what they refuse to face.
 
Their comfort with mediocrity. 
Their constant explanations. 
Their fear disguised as “being realistic.”
 
Watching someone settle is clarifying.
 
It shows you exactly how ambition dies: 
Not all at once, but over time and convincingly.
 
Not everyone is meant to be a role model. 
Some are cautionary tales.
 
Let that be useful.
 
Let it remind you to: 

• choose growth over comfort 
• action over excuses 
• courage over familiarity
 
You don’t owe anyone loyalty to the limits they accepted.
 
Rise anyway.
