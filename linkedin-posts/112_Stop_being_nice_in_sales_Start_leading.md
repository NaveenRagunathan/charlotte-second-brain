Stop being 'nice' in sales. Start leading.

Early in my business journey, 
I thought being nice would win clients.

I’d say things like:
❌ "Let me know what you think!"
❌ "If you’re interested, we can chat."

I believed if I gave prospects space..
They’d come back when they were ready.

They didn’t.

Instead, I got:
Ghosted in the DMs.
“I need to think about it.”
Prospects who never took action.

As a coach or consultant, your job isn’t to wait;
It’s to confidently guide prospects to a decision.

They need you to be their expert.
Challenge them to take action

I'm not saying be unkind here. 
Am saying be direct from a place of love.

Confident closers: 
✅ Ask direct questions.
✅ Lead the conversation.
✅ Don’t let hesitation stall the sale.
✅ Handle objections before they come up.

When I stopped waiting and started leading, 
Everything changed.

What are you doing to LEAD to win clients?
