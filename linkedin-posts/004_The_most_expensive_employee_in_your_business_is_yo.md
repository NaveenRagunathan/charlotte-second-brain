Here’s how to build yours:

1/ Create a Founder Sales Project

Keep your offer, positioning, buyer profiles, sales process, call notes, messaging, objections, and follow-up in one place.

2/ Upload your strongest sales material

Add your best DMs, proposals, testimonials, call transcripts, closed-deal notes, and buyer questions.

Real examples teach Claude how your sales process works.

3/ Document your ideal buyer

Include their goals, pressures, buying triggers, previous attempts, common objections, and the language they use.

4/ Add your offer and positioning

Explain the problem you solve, the outcome you create, your method, your proof, and who gets the strongest result.

5/ Train it on your sales voice

Upload examples of how you speak and write.

Add simple rules around tone, structure, research, questions, and next steps.

6/ Build a prospect research workflow
Ask Claude to review the prospect’s profile, company, content, priorities, and buying signals.

Then identify the most relevant reason to start a conversation.

7/ Create five reusable sales workflows

Build one for:

→ buyer research 
→ outbound messaging 
→ discovery preparation 
→ objection handling 
→ follow-up and call debriefs

8/ Feed it real conversations
Add meeting transcripts, call notes, emails, and DM threads.

Ask:
Where did trust increase? 
Where did momentum drop? 
What remained unclear? 
What should happen next?

9/ Save every correction
Turn weak outputs into new rules.
Each correction makes the system sharper.

10/ Update it monthly
Add new wins, stronger messages, fresh objections, better questions, and clearer positioning.
